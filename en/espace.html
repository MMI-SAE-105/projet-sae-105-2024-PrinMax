The world and space

In 2154, the world view on space a subi une transformation radicale, passant d'un rêve lointain d'exploration à une réalité intergalactique concrète. Les avancées technologiques in both science and engineering, have enabled mankind to achieve feats that were that were once considered science fiction. The colonization of Mars and several moons of Jupiter and Saturn illustrate this new era of exploration. These once hostile environments have been transformed into sustainable habitats, thanks to innovative infrastructures and advanced farming systems using techniques such as hydroponics and aquaponics.

This new era of space exploration also brought about a change in the way nations and companies and companies perceive and manage space. International cooperation has become essential, with alliances formed to share the resources and knowledge needed to exploit the resources spatiales. Space stations and their evolution

Satellites and space stations orbit have become hubs for scientific research and innovation. and innovation. These facilities house state-of-the-art laboratories where scientists from all over the world collaborate on ambitious projects, ranging from the study of the effects of the microgravity on human biology to the observation of climate change on earth.

In conclusion

Space is no longer seen as a frontier to be conquered, but as a new horizon of possibilities for humanity. This collective vision embodies a future where sustainability, exploration and discovery coexist, enriching our understanding of the universe. Humanity, united in this common quest, pledges to protect its ecosystemwhile broadening its horizons, seeking to establish colonies on other worlds while preserving Earth. This vision of Space in 2154 testifies to the human aspiration to explore, understand and live in harmony with the universethat around us.
